Pathology Analytical Imaging Standards (PAIS) data management system provides a framework to model, manage and query analytical results and human annotations of pathology images, along with whole slide images. To find more information on PAIS, please go to following wikis:
  • PAIS Wiki
  • PIDB Wiki
You can go to PAIS demo Web Portal to browse, visualize and download images and analytical results or annotations. The demo dataset includes 480 whole slide images with GBMs from TCGA Portal.

You can download images and analytical results for low grade glioma datasets.
